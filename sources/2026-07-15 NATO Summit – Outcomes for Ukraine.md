The Ukrainian Ministry of Defence has provided information on the outcomes of the 36th NATO Summit. Under the agreements reached, the Alliance will provide the following assistance:

➡️NATO will allocate $80 billion to Ukraine, with the same amount to be allocated next year.

➡️Norway has allocated $306 million for the purchase of Patriot air defence missiles. These will be sourced from existing stocks and from new production.

➡️Germany will manufacture Ukrainian ‘Bars’ missiles.

➡️Three agreements on unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) have been signed. Ukraine will develop drone production in partnership with Estonia, the Netherlands and Denmark.

➡️The DSRB bank is being established with funding of up to $134 billion. Representatives from Canada, Albania, Belgium, Greece, Latvia, Luxembourg, Romania and Turkey have announced their intention to establish the bank.
