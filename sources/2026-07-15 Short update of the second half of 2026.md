Brace yourselves for a very bumpy second half of 2026: US-Israel-Iran escalations will continue with no end in sight: Yemen-Saudi Arabia tensions coming; Türkiye & Syria might get also involved. Russia will face strong recession scenario due to the war in Iran and will boost the summer offensive, while Ukraine will seek to quickly build antiballistic missile defence systems ahead of the winter. The Indo-Pacific will face further militarizations and tensions. Two two needed (real) ceasefires in Ukraine and Iran are the mutually strengthening signal towards preventing the Global System Rupture scenario or the fourth systemic risk-driven global crisis.
